About

Art That Feels Personal

Eager to learn and always happy to help, I left the 9–5 life to follow my passion for art and business — and to set an example for my two boys. I want them to grow up treating people with kindness, staying humble, and going after what truly matters to them.

I hold very high standards when it comes to my work because every tattoo I create is permanent and personal. I’m grateful for every client who trusts me with their ideas. I’m not a judgmental artist, but I’ll always give my honest opinion when it comes to the structure, flow, and overall look of a tattoo so you leave with something you’ll love for life.

L5 stands for Love the Change. Based out of Supernatural Tattoo Studio TX, my goal is to create tattoos that bring confidence, peace, and a real sense of meaning every time you look at them.

“Empty your mind. Be formless, like water. Flow or crash.”
